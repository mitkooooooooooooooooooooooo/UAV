UAV INTERCEPTOR: DUBAI C-RAM
Sector: Al Marmoom, Dubai | Unit: LPWS C-RAM

A high-stakes browser defense simulator where you operate a Land-
Based Phalanx Weapon System to protect the city from automated

threats.

1. Operation
Aim: Move your mouse to rotate the turret.
Fire: Hold Left-Click to engage targets.
Cost: Each shot costs $80 . Monitor the budget in the Tactical
Report.

2. Tactical Logic (The "Pi")

Automaton Behavior:
The Drones and Rockets act as simple Automata following a 3-
step loop:
Start: Appear above the city.
Act: Drones move side-to-side; Rockets fall fast.
Check: The system constantly checks for bullet hits or
ground impacts.

3. Setup
Ensure the following files are in the same folder:

/project-folder
├── index.html
├── main.js
└── dubai-skyline.png

4. Mission Objectives
Victory: 200 Interceptions.
Defeat: 50 Impacts on base integrity.
